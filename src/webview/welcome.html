Quick Start

GitLiveLog helps you track your coding journey by automatically logging your development activities. Here's how to get started:

  1. Click "Start Tracking" to begin monitoring your coding activity
  2. GitLiveLog will automatically commit your changes every 30 minutes (configurable)
  3. Your activity will be logged to a dedicated GitHub repository

Configuration

Customize GitLiveLog in your VS Code settings:

  • gitlivelog.repoName: Name of your tracking repository
  • gitlivelog.commitFrequency: How often to commit (in minutes)
  • gitlivelog.exclude: Patterns to exclude from tracking
  • gitlivelog.showCommitDialog: Show confirmation before commits
